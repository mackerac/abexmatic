About Us

ABEXMATIC HYDRAULICS PVT LTD

Abexmatic Hydraulics Pvt. Ltd established in 1991 is an ISO 9001/2008 Certified Delhi based Company, with its forte lying in manufacturing Hydraulic presses and Machines as per the client’s need. The company is manufacturing all types of high precision Hydraulic Presses, all kinds of Hydraulic Cylinders and Power Packs, CNC Injection Moulding Machines, Pet Stretch Blow Moulding Machines and the like, for decades.
The management and operations of the company is run through the hands of proficient and qualified mechanical engineers, long-employed for our both factories located in Industrial areas of Mundka as well as Bawana in ambit of Delhi. Our each manufactory is spread on an area of 10,000 Sq ft.
Abexmatic Hydraulics Pvt. Ltd boasts a well-equipped and updated workshop essential for manufacturing quality Hydraulics Machines by our qualified engineers, Auto Cad designers, experienced workmen and up-to-date manufacturing facilities for mechanizing unsurpassed machinery as per our customer’s requirements.
The company supplies all types of Hydraulic Presses and Cylinders across India and abroad, as per the client’s requirement.

Company Profile

Spread at area of over 10,000 Sqft. Its infrastructure comprises contemporary tools most suited for making Hydraulic Presses along with all the latest machines installed such as C.N.C , C.N.C Boring, Hydraulic Planer, Radial Drills, Mill Precision grinders, Precision drill machines, Cutting & welding equipment to name a few, which are required for manufacturing impeccable Hydraulic presses. All the work area in our factories is equipped with Heavy Duty E.O/Overhead cranes for facilitating heavy machineries/materials safe movement Today, our vast experience, consistent quality up gradations & continuous R&D has made our products at par with the global standards. Utmost care is taken to ensure that Quality Standards are maintained throughout the manufacturing process. The company is supplying all types of Hydraulic Presses and Cylinders throughout India and abroad.

Quality Policy

Abexmatic Hydraulics Pvt. Ltd. manufactures the Hydraulic Presses as per prevailing Indian & international standards. The company is certified by I.S.O – 9001/2008 certifying bodies & is equipped with all latest quality control equipments/instruments to meet the accuracy of the presses to the highest standards. The presses produced by us undergo rigorous endurance tests for its full capacity prior to delivery with our aim to provide machines featuring safe and correct operating functions installed that meet exact industry requirements resulting in valuable satisfied customer experience.Most of our presses conforms to IS:14877( part-1)- 2000, grade 1 to 3 standards depending upon customer requirements We guarantee all our customers that our manufactured hydraulic presses pass strict and complete quality controls. Our timely after-sale services are also dedicated to fulfill the customer’s hour of need.
