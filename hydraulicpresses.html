Hydraulic Presses

4-Column Type Precision Cold Forging Hydraulic Presses

The traditionally designed 4 column Hydraulic Presses with reinforced steel structure are made for cold forging precision Auto / Industrial components in mass production with minimum rejection and maximum tool life through minimum deflection, optimum hydraulic circuit design and integrated control of manufacturing process

H Frame Type Cold Forging Hydraulic Presses

Also known as Straight sided Hydraulic ress or Gib guided presses are placed in the most demanding applications. Ruggedly Constructed with industry superior eight point gibbing, straight sided presses ensure millions of cycles and extended tool wear.

Deep Drawing Hydraulic Press

The Deep Drawing Hydraulic Presses are specially designed for best performance, equipped with PLC and LVDT depth control while being highly rigid, compact and space saving design. These machines are widely used in manufacturing SS Sinks, LPG cylinders, Geysers, Air Conditioners, Cooking Hobs and other Automobile and Industrial Sheet metal components.

Multi Purpose Hydraulic Presses

These presses are suitable for drawing, coining, trimming, punching, stamping and other general operations. These presses are fitted with PLC and LVDT for precision control and are highly rigid and compact in design and can be fitted with die cushioning arrangement for deep drawing.

Hydraulic Elbow Cold Forging Machine

These elbow cold forming machines are designed for manufacturing stainless steel, carbon steel and alloy steel elbows with curvature of 1D to 1.5D. the products are widely used in the connection of the industrial pipe lines for patro chemical, gas, oil pipes, electrical power, food industries.
